The Entheocast Podcast

A research-first audio series on psychedelic medicine. Each episode goes deep on a single study, trial, or regulatory development — with the researchers, clinicians, and policy experts who are doing the work.

What to expect
  • Weekly 30-minute episodes tied to the same data as the tracker
  • Trial readouts with the principal investigators
  • Regulatory analysis — FDA, DEA, EMA, Health Canada
  • Compound deep-dives: mechanism, pharmacology, clinical evidence
  • No wellness hype. No trip reports. Peer-reviewed sources only.

No spam. One email when the first episode drops.
