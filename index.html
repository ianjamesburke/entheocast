RFK, Jr., puts psychedelics on fast track to FDA review and approval

The FDA announced it is fast-tracking review and approval for three companies studying psilocybin and methylone for depression and PTSD, and approved a phase 1 clinical trial of noribogaine for alcohol use disorder.
